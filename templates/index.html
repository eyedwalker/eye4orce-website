Appointment Communication

Opt In for Appointment Reminders

Stay connected with Eye4orce for important appointment reminders and updates via SMS.

SMS Terms and Conditions
Message and Data Rates May Apply

Standard message and data rates may apply. Please check with your carrier for details.

Opt-In Requirements

To opt-in to receive SMS updates from Eye4orce, you must:

  • Be 18 years of age or older
  • Provide a valid mobile phone number
  • Agree to receive appointment-related messages from Eye4orce
Message Types

You will receive SMS messages for the following appointment-related purposes:

  • Initial appointment confirmation
  • 24-hour appointment reminders
  • 2-hour appointment reminders
  • Appointment cancellation instructions
  • Rescheduling notifications
  • Important updates about your appointment
Message Frequency

While you may receive up to 4 messages per month about general updates, appointment-related messages will be sent as needed:

  • Up to 3 messages per appointment (confirmation, 24-hour reminder, 2-hour reminder)
  • Additional messages may be sent for rescheduling or important updates
  • Messages will be sent at appropriate times before your appointment
Opt-Out Instructions

To unsubscribe from SMS messages at any time, simply reply with the word STOP to any message. You will then receive a confirmation message confirming your unsubscription.

Please note: Unsubscribing will prevent you from receiving important appointment reminders and updates. We recommend keeping your subscription active to ensure you don't miss your appointments.

Help and Support

To get help with SMS messages, reply with the word HELP to any message. You will receive instructions on how to manage your subscription.

For additional support, please contact us at support@eye4orce.com or call our appointment line at (512) 266-2020.

Important Notes

By opting in, you acknowledge that:

  • Appointment-related messages are important for managing your care
  • Message frequency may increase around appointment times
  • Missing appointment reminders could result in missed appointments
  • We reserve the right to send additional messages for important updates
